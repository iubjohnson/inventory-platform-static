Billing questions

Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial?

Yes — every plan includes a 14-day free trial. You can install Stockwik, connect your store, and run a full reorder plan before you're charged anything.

What's the difference between the plans?

Starter is for planning — see what to buy and tune your reorder logic in Stockwik. Growth adds the ability to act on it in Shopify: create and send POs, receive stock, run cycle counts, write costs back, and the full reports suite. Pro adds multi-location inventory, stock transfers, and location-aware planning. You can change plans anytime.

Do you charge setup or onboarding fees?

No. There are no setup or onboarding fees on any plan — install from the Shopify App Store, use the 14-day free trial, and only pay once it ends.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Billing is month-to-month with no annual lock-in. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your current billing period.

How does billing work with Shopify?

Stockwik bills through Shopify's standard app billing, so your subscription appears on your regular Shopify invoice alongside your other apps.
