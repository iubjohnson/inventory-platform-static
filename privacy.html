Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how [Legal Entity Name] ("Stockwik," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information in connection with the Stockwik inventory planning application for Shopify (the "App"), the Stockwik website at stockwik.com (the "Site"), and related services (together, the "Services").

By installing the App or using the Services, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

Who this applies to

Our customers are Shopify merchants and the people who use the App on their behalf. The App also processes data drawn from a merchant's Shopify store. Where we process store or customer data on a merchant's behalf, we act as a processor (or "service provider") and the merchant is the controller of that data.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, store name, and account settings.
  • Contact and support messages — when you contact us (for example, through the contact form on the Site), we receive your name, email address, and the contents of your message.
  • Billing — subscriptions are billed through Shopify. We receive subscription and billing status from Shopify, but we do not collect or store your full payment card details.

Store data from Shopify

When you connect your Shopify store, the App accesses data through Shopify's APIs so it can plan and manage your inventory. This may include products and variants, inventory levels and locations, order and sales history, supplier and cost information, and related store records. We use this data to provide the Services to you, as described below.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and log data — such as IP address, browser and device type, pages or features accessed, and timestamps.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — used to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand how the Services are used. See "Cookies" below.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services — including demand forecasting, reorder suggestions, purchase orders, receiving, counts, transfers, reporting, and writing inventory updates back to Shopify.
  • Authenticate users, process subscriptions, and provide customer support.
  • Monitor, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the Services.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and service-related notices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described here:

  • Service providers (subprocessors) — third parties that help us run the Services, listed below. They may process data only on our instructions and for the purposes of providing their service to us.
  • Legal and safety — when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Stockwik, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
