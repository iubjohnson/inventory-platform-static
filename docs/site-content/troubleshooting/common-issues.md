# Common issues

Fixes for the handful of things that occasionally trip people up.

## My numbers look out of date

Syncing is manual, so run a **Sync** (top of the app) to refresh products, inventory, and sales from Shopify. See [How syncing works](../concepts/how-syncing-works).

## "Your store is disconnected" / "Reconnect your store"

The secure link to Shopify needs re-establishing (you uninstalled the app, or a connection lapsed). Click **Reconnect store** — your data is safe. See [Reconnecting your store](../account/reconnecting-your-store).

## My PO won't email

Set your **company email** in **Settings → Company profile** (it's used as the reply-to), and make sure the **supplier** has an email address. Then resend. You can always use **PDF / Print** instead. See [Sending a PO](../purchase-orders/sending-a-po).

## A count line was rejected when I committed

A sale came in for that product *while you were counting*, so its on-hand changed. Stockwik shows you the fresh number to re-verify, then you commit that line again — the rest of your counts still went through. See [Cycle counts & adjustments](../counts/cycle-counts-and-adjustments).

## My import won't apply

Imports are **all-or-nothing**, so one bad row blocks the batch. The preview tells you exactly which rows have errors — fix those and re-upload. Re-running a corrected file is safe. See [Import & export inventory](../inventory/import-export-inventory).

## A feature shows a lock (🔒)

It's not in your current plan. See [Plans & features](../account/plans-and-features) to compare tiers, or [Changing your plan](../account/changing-your-plan) to upgrade.

## I'm stuck on a full-screen message

Plan and reconnect screens always include a **Sign out** link, so you can switch accounts if needed.
