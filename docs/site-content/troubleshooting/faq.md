# FAQ

Quick answers to the questions merchants ask most.

## Why am I seeing "Choose a plan to continue"?

Your workspace doesn't have an active subscription (or the trial lapsed). Pick a plan to continue — every plan starts with a 14-day free trial. See [Choosing a plan](../getting-started/choosing-a-plan).

## Why is a feature locked (the 🔒)?

That feature isn't included in your current plan. Purchase orders, receiving, counts, and reports are on **Growth and up**; transfers and multi-location are on **Pro**. See [Plans & features](../account/plans-and-features).

## Why is a product's reorder suggestion 0?

It's well-stocked — "Watch" or "Healthy" — so it doesn't need ordering yet. You can still type a quantity to add it to an order. See [Understanding suggestions](../reordering/understanding-suggestions).

## Why did a reorder suggestion suddenly jump up?

Usually a **seasonal** product entering its peak window — Stockwik raises the target so you're stocked for the busy period. See [How sales velocity works](../concepts/how-sales-velocity-works).

## How far back does Stockwik look at sales?

A rolling **full year** of order history, used for velocity and reporting. See [How syncing works](../concepts/how-syncing-works).

## How often does Stockwik sync?

Syncing is **manual** today — click **Sync** whenever you want fresh numbers. The counts page also refreshes inventory automatically when you open it.

## Does Stockwik change my Shopify inventory?

Only when **you** receive a purchase order, commit a count, or edit a cost — and Shopify always stays the source of truth. Stockwik never changes your stock on its own. See [Inventory: on hand, available & committed](../concepts/inventory-on-hand-available-committed).

## Can I connect more than one Shopify store?

Each workspace connects to one store. For multiple stores, set up a separate workspace per store. See [Workspace & team](../account/workspace-and-team).
