# Changing your plan

You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Plan changes go through Shopify, and new features unlock as soon as the change is confirmed.

## How to change plans

1. Go to **Settings → Plan & billing**.
2. Click **Change plan** (or **Choose a plan** if you don't have an active subscription).
3. You're taken to **Shopify's secure pricing page**. Pick your new plan and confirm.
4. You're returned to Stockwik with the new plan applied. If it doesn't update right away, click **Refresh** on the Plan & billing page.

## Upgrades and downgrades

- **Upgrading** unlocks the new features immediately (e.g. moving to Growth turns on purchase orders, receiving, counts, and reports).
- **Downgrading** takes effect per Shopify's billing; features above your new tier become locked, but your data is never deleted — re-upgrading restores access.

## Your trial

Every plan includes a **14-day free trial**, handled by Shopify — you won't be charged until it ends. Changing plans during the trial is fine.

> **Tip:** If you ever change your plan directly in Shopify's admin, open **Settings → Plan & billing** in Stockwik and hit **Refresh** so the app picks up the change right away.
