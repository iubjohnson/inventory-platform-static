# Settings: Plan & billing

This is where you see your current plan and make changes. All billing runs through Shopify.
## What you'll see

- Your **current plan** (Starter / Growth / Pro) and **billing status** (active or trialing).
- Your **trial end date**, if you're in the 14-day free trial.

## Actions

- **Change plan** (or **Choose a plan** if you don't have an active one) — opens Shopify's secure pricing page to upgrade, downgrade, or subscribe.
- **Refresh** — re-checks your subscription with Shopify. Handy if you just changed plans and want the app to reflect it immediately.

> **Note:** Billing, plan changes, and your trial are all handled by **Shopify** — Stockwik never sees your payment details. Upgrades unlock their features as soon as the change is confirmed.
