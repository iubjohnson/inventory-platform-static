# Settings: Company profile

Your company profile is the business information that appears on the purchase orders you send to suppliers.

`[SCREENSHOT: Settings → Company profile]`

## Fields

- **Business name**
- **Email** — used as the reply-to on emailed POs (so suppliers can reach you).
- **Phone**
- **Address** (line 1, line 2, city, state/region, postal code, country)

Click **Save** to apply.

## Where it shows up

These details form the **"from"** block on every purchase order — printed or emailed. The **"ship to"** address, by contrast, comes from the destination location (see [Locations](../settings/locations)).

> **Note:** Set your **email** before sending POs by email — it's used as the reply-to address. Without it, the email option won't work.
