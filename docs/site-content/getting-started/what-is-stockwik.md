# What is Stockwik?

Stockwik is inventory and replenishment software for Shopify merchants who fulfill from a single hub. It learns how your products actually sell and tells you exactly what to reorder, when, and how much — then helps you raise purchase orders, receive stock, run counts, and (on multi-location plans) move inventory between locations, all in one fast app. It's the demand-planning brain Shopify leaves out, without the spreadsheets.

## Who it's for

Stockwik is built for small and mid-sized Shopify businesses that fulfill orders from one main hub (with optional extra locations). If you've been managing reorders in a spreadsheet, eyeballing what to buy, or wrestling with Shopify's basic stock tools, Stockwik is designed to replace that with something faster and smarter.

## The core loop

Using Stockwik is a simple, repeatable cycle:

1. **Sync** your Shopify store — products, inventory, sales, and locations come in automatically.
2. **See what to buy.** Stockwik watches your sales velocity and flags exactly which products need reordering, and how much.
3. **Order.** Turn those suggestions into purchase orders in a couple of clicks, grouped by supplier, then email or print them.
4. **Receive.** When stock arrives, receive it against the PO — your Shopify inventory updates automatically.
5. **Count.** Run cycle counts to keep your numbers honest, with discrepancies pushed back to Shopify.

Everything is driven by your real sales history, so the more you sell, the smarter the suggestions get.

## What makes it different

- **It tells you what to buy** — not just what you have. Velocity-based reorder points and order-up-to levels mean you stop guessing.
- **Shopify stays the source of truth.** Stockwik mirrors your Shopify stock and only writes back when you deliberately receive, count, or update a cost — so your numbers stay consistent.
- **Fast and focused.** One app for reordering, purchase orders, receiving, counts, transfers, and reporting — no spreadsheet sprawl.
