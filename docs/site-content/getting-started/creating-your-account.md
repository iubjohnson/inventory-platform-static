# Creating your account

After you've chosen a plan, you'll create your Stockwik account. Most of the form is already filled in from your Shopify store, so it only takes a moment.

## Sign up

1. Stockwik pre-fills your **email**, **company name**, and **name** from your Shopify store profile. Adjust anything if needed.
2. Choose a **password**.
3. Click to create your account.

`[SCREENSHOT: signup form]`

## Verify your email

For security, Stockwik emails you a **6-digit verification code**. Enter it to confirm your email and finish creating your account.

`[SCREENSHOT: email verification code step]`

> **Tip:** Didn't get the code? Check your spam folder, and make sure the email address shown is the one you can access. You can request a new code if it doesn't arrive.

## Your workspace

Once verified, Stockwik automatically creates your **workspace** — named from your company — and links it to your Shopify store. From there you go straight into the [onboarding walkthrough](../getting-started/onboarding-walkthrough).

> **Note:** Each Stockwik workspace connects to **one** Shopify store. If you run more than one store, you'd set up a separate workspace for each. See [Workspace & team](../account/workspace-and-team).
