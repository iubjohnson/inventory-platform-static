Workspace & team

Your workspace is your Stockwik account. It's connected to one Shopify store and holds all your suppliers, buy options, purchase orders, counts, and settings.

One store, one workspace

Each workspace links to a single Shopify store. This keeps your data clean and your planning focused on one store's demand.

If you operate more than one Shopify store, you'd set up a separate workspace for each — they stay fully independent.

Inviting your team

Open your account menu (your name in the navigation) to manage your profile and invite teammates to your workspace, so your buyer, warehouse staff, and bookkeeper can all work in Stockwik.

Signing out

You can sign out from the same account menu. If you're ever stopped at a full-screen message (for example, a plan or reconnect screen), there's a Sign out link right on it — so you're never stuck.

Note
Your login is separate from your Shopify login. Signing out of Stockwik doesn't affect your Shopify session.
