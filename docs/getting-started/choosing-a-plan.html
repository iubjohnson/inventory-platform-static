Choosing a plan

Right after you install, Stockwik asks you to pick a plan. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial, and all billing is handled securely through Shopify.

The three plans

PlanPriceBest for
Starter$29/moKnowing what to buy — inventory visibility and reorder suggestions (view-only).
Growth$39/moClosing the loop — purchase orders, receiving, counts, cost edits, and reports.
Pro$49/moRunning multiple locations — everything in Growth plus stock transfers.

For the full breakdown of what each plan unlocks, see Plans & features.

How it works

  1. On the plan-selection screen, choose the plan that fits.
  2. You're taken to Shopify's secure pricing page to confirm. Your 14-day free trial starts here — you won't be charged until it ends.
  3. Once confirmed, you continue to account setup.
Note
Billing, plan changes, and your trial are all managed by Shopify — Stockwik never handles your payment details. You can change plans anytime from Settings → Plan & billing.
Tip
Not sure which to pick? Start with Growth — it's the plan most merchants need (it unlocks purchase orders, receiving, and reports), and you can downgrade or upgrade later during or after the trial.
