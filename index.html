Why Stockwik

Purchasing decisions you can actually trust.

Stockwik builds your reorder plan from your real sales history, then tunes it with the inputs only you know — supplier lead times, target days of cover, and safety stock. The result isn't a generic guess. It's a plan that reflects how your store actually runs.

Built from your numbers

Reorder points come from your sales velocity combined with your lead times, days-of-cover target, and safety stock. Set them once, override any product, and the plan adapts.

Purchasing built in

Not just analytics. Create, send, and receive purchase orders — grouped by vendor, rounded to pack sizes, with cost tracking and a full audit trail.

Keep inventory accurate

Receiving, transfers between your Shopify locations, and scheduled cycle counts all write stock straight back to Shopify — so your counts update the moment goods land, move, or get recounted, and your store reflects what's really on the shelf.

How it works

From sales data to signed purchase order.

STEP 01

Connect your store

Stockwik syncs your products, variants, inventory levels, locations, and full sales history into one clean dataset. Setup takes minutes.

STEP 02

Get your buy plan

It computes demand from recency-weighted sales velocity, derives reorder points, nets out current stock and open POs, and tells you what to buy.

STEP 03

Send the orders

Review the plan, grouped by supplier and rounded to pack sizes. Send POs by email or PDF, receive shipments, and write stock back to Shopify.
