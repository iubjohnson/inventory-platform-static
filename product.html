Demand forecasting

Velocity that reflects
what's selling now.

Stockwik computes a recency-weighted "blended" sales velocity for every product — so a recent spike counts more than last quarter's lull. It detects seasonality and tracks peak demand, giving you a forecast you can act on instead of a flat average.

Reorder points

Reorder points tuned
to how you buy.

Stockwik calculates a reorder point and an order-up-to level for every product — and you set the inputs that make them fit your business: supplier lead times, safety stock, and your target days of cover. Tune them per product or supplier so the numbers match how you actually operate. Disagree with a forecast? Override it and Stockwik respects your call.

Purchase orders

From buy plan to PO
in one click.

Stockwik nets out your current stock and open POs, rounds to supplier pack sizes and minimum order quantities, and groups everything by vendor into draft purchase orders — or start a one-off PO any time you need to. Review, edit, and send each one to your supplier by email or PDF.

Receiving

Receive shipments,
stock updates itself.

When goods arrive, check them in against the PO — in full or in part, with backorders tracked on whatever's still owed. Stockwik writes the received quantities straight back to Shopify, captures real landed cost per unit, and logs every receipt to an audit trail, so your on-hand and your margins are right the moment the boxes land.

Inventory transfers

Move stock to
where it sells.

When one location is selling out while another sits on surplus, rebalance instead of over-buying. Build a transfer between your Shopify locations, send it, and Stockwik updates inventory at both the origin and destination in Shopify when it's received — with the same audit trail as a PO.

Cycle counts

Keep your on-hand
numbers honest.

Every forecast and reorder point rests on accurate stock. Count a slice of your catalog on a schedule instead of shutting the store for an annual count — enter or scan counts, see the variance against Shopify at a glance, and post adjustments that sync straight back. Small, regular counts keep your numbers from drifting.

Reporting

The numbers behind
every decision.

Stockwik ships a full reporting suite across four areas — inventory & valuation, sales & profitability, purchasing & suppliers, and operations & audit. Dig into dead-stock and overstock value, ABC analysis, gross margin, supplier lead-time and fill rate, cycle-count shrinkage, and a complete movement ledger. Set any date range, then export to CSV or PDF.

Pack sizes

Truly flexible
buy levels.

Few suppliers sell one unit at a time. Define the pack types you actually order in — case, box, pallet, or your own custom unit — and set how many units each one holds. Stockwik turns its suggested quantities into whole packs, respects each supplier's minimum order quantity, and builds the PO in the units your vendor ships, so what you order is what they can fulfill.
