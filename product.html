what's selling now.

Stockwik computes a recency-weighted "blended" sales velocity for every product — so a recent spike counts more than last quarter's lull. It detects seasonality and tracks peak demand, giving you a forecast you can act on instead of a flat average.

Reorder points

The math, shown —
not hidden.

Stockwik derives a reorder point from sales velocity × (lead time + safety stock), and an order-up-to level from your days-of-cover target. Every number is visible and editable. Disagree with a forecast? Override it per product and Stockwik respects your call.

Purchase orders

From buy plan to PO

Stockwik nets out your current stock and open POs, rounds to supplier pack sizes and minimum order quantities, and groups everything by vendor into draft purchase orders. Edit, email, or print to PDF, then receive shipments with stock written back to Shopify and a full audit trail.
